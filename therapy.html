Registration process

A simple counselling enquiry route.

This gives the team a clear process to refine later when the formal counselling registration flow is ready.

1. Choose the closest support type

Start with individual, couples, family, group, online or creative therapy. If you are unsure, choose the closest fit.

2. Send one enquiry

Use the contact form or WhatsApp and include your name, area, preferred therapy type and whether support is urgent.

3. The team routes you

Floating Counselling Community reviews eligibility, availability, safeguarding needs and whether online or in-person care is best.

Therapy questions

Counselling, clearly explained.

These answers help people, carers and referrers understand what support is available before they contact us.

What therapy does Floating Counselling Community offer?

Floating Counselling Community offers one-to-one, couple, family, group, online and in-person therapy for people who need practical, trauma-informed care.

Can I access low-cost counselling?

Floating Counselling Community provides free and low-cost care for eligible clients, including elderly, Universal Credit and low-income clients.

How do I register interest for counselling?

Use the contact form or WhatsApp the team with your preferred therapy type, location and whether online or in-person support would work best.

Which areas do you support?

We support people across Croydon, Redbridge, Newham, Durham and Southwark, with online and in-person options depending on the service and location.
