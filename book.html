What the book covers

Discipline that protects attachment.

"Connect before you correct, discipline with love, and still create a secure attachment."
  • 01 Understand why physical punishment harms trust and emotional safety.
  • 02 Build self-awareness around parenting triggers and inherited patterns.
  • 03 Use practical, bonding-focused strategies shaped by psychotherapy and Christian values.
  • 04 Apply the PACE model: playfulness, acceptance, curiosity and empathy.
